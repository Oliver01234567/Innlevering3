Welcome to the Game Tutorial

Getting Started

In this game, the objective is to get the best pets, finish all the achievements and upgrading everything to the max

You get money from destroying the coins spread around the map. Each coin also has a chance of dropping diamonds

How to Play

Move around with either "wasd" or the arrow keys

Click on the coins around the map to send your pets to attack them. If you click on a coin while all your pets are away, they will all come back to you

Buy eggs to get new pets

Advanced Tips

Every minute you can spin the wheel to get diamonds with the "spin" button

By upgrading the coins, coins will give more money, but they will also get more health

If you want to delete multiple pets at once, you can use the "Multi Delete" button in the inventory

You can open the menu by pressing "m"
